taizai //

foreword

Taizai is a series of photos from my semester abroad in Japan.

This collection is not meant to serve as a full travel log,
but to showcase the beauty I have found in some of my surroundings.

That said, I have worked to balance shots of nice places I've visited
amongst the slightly more experimental photos.

Kyoto Wanderer: A late-winter trip to Kyoto.

Spring Procession: A collection of shots from the ephemeral cherry blossom season.

It Flies By: A return to Tokyo during Golden Week.

Farewell Tour: A circle down to Kochi and up to Okayama.

Ode to Nagoya: Another home-away-from-home.

Completed July 12 2023
