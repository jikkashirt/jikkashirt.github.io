foreword

Taizai is an ongoing series of photos from my semester abroad in Japan.

This collection is not meant to serve as a full travel log,
but to showcase the beauty I have found in some of my surroundings.

That said, I have worked to balance shots of nice places I've visited
amongst the slightly more experimental photos.

Kyoto Wanderer attempts to capture the sense of adventure
I felt during my early February trip, as well as offering a variety of scenes
from Kyoto's cityscape and its iconic temples.

Spring Procession is a collection of shots from the ephemeral cherry blossom season.
It also calls back to the notion of seishun, the springtime of youth,
as students start another year under the pink flowers.

It Flies By features photos taken during my trip to Tokyo during Golden Week.
Returning after four years, I visited places both new and old,
once again getting lost in the largest metropolitan area on the planet.

Farewell Tour marks the farthest out of my comfort zone I have gone
in terms of solo traveling. With less than two weeks left of my stay, I went down to Kochi
and made my way around some famous castles.
